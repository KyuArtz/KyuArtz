Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What type of artwork do you specialize in?

    I specialize in digital art, focusing on character illustrations, comics, and concept art. My style ranges from fantasy themes to modern, vibrant designs.

    2. Do you accept commission requests?

    Yes, I do accept commissions! Please check my Commission Sheet for more information on pricing, types of commissions, and how to place an order.

    3. How long does it take to complete a commission?

    The time required to complete a commission varies depending on the complexity of the piece and my current workload. Typically, it takes 1-3 weeks for smaller projects, while larger or more detailed pieces may take up to a month or more.

    4. What payment methods do you accept?

    I accept payments via PayPal, Maya, and Gcash. Payment is required either in full upfront or split into two parts: 50% upfront and the remaining 50% upon completion, depending on the project.

    5. Can I use your art for commercial purposes?

    My art is for personal use only unless a commercial license has been negotiated. For more information on terms and usage, please refer to my Terms of Service .

    6. How can I contact you for collaborations?

    You can reach out to me through my email via kyuushi992@gmail.com . I'm open to collaboration requests depending on the project details and schedule.

    7. Do you offer refunds?

    Refunds are typically not offered once the artwork has been started. However, if I am unable to complete the commission for any reason, a full or partial refund may be provided depending on the circumstances. Please check the Terms of Service for more details.

    8. Can I request revisions after the commission is completed?

    Yes, I allow up to two minor revisions after the artwork is completed. However, significant changes may incur additional charges. It's important to provide detailed feedback during the sketch phase to avoid major changes later on.
