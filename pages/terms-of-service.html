Terms of Service

  • Accepted Payment Methods: The accepted payment methods are PayPal, Maya, or Gcash.
  • Payment Policy: To avoid potential scams, clients are required to make full payment upfront before receiving the finalized product. Payments must be made within the agreed-upon timeframe.
  • Revision Policy: Revisions are unlimited until the client is satisfied. However, significant changes or additional work may incur extra charges. Each revision request must be submitted within the specified timeframe after receiving the previous draft.
  • Refund Policy: Refunds are not available unless the commission cannot be completed due to unforeseen circumstances on the artist's end.
  • Price Negotiation: Prices can be negotiated based on the client's budget.
  • Right to Reject Commissions: I reserve the right to reject any commission that I find uncomfortable or unsuitable.
  • Intellectual Property Rights: The artist retains all rights to the commissioned artwork. Clients are granted a personal-use license. For commercial use, pricing will be higher, and proper credit must be given to the artist.
  • Reference Requirements: Clients must provide clear and detailed references or descriptions for their commissioned art requests.
  • Progress Updates: I will provide updates, including sketches, base coloring, and rendering, at various stages of the process.
  • Price Adjustments for Complexity: For multiple characters or detailed backgrounds, the overall price will be adjusted accordingly from the original quote.
  • Credit Requirement: Clients must credit me when sharing the commissioned work. Your acknowledgment is greatly appreciated.
  • Possible Delays: Please anticipate potential delays or slower responses for your commissioned art request, as I am a working student with multiple clients. Your understanding is appreciated.
  • Prohibition on NFTs and AI: The sale of my commissioned works as NFTs or their use in AI-generated programs is explicitly prohibited.
  • Delivery Method: The completed commissioned artwork will be delivered via Gmail or Google Drive.
  • Contact Information: If you have any issues or questions, feel free to contact me at kyuushi992@gmail.com . I typically respond within 48 hours.
