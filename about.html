SUMMARY

I'm Kyu shi, a self-taught freelance artist, and I've been drawing since I was a young child. I taught myself how to draw and tried to improve myself gradually. In my spare time, I always draw to further improve my drawing abilities and experience in art. Even though I'm still learning by myself, I never give up, and after 4 years of drawing traditionally, I could see improvements. Then, I switched to digital because it caught my attention bacause it was popular among the social media platforms nowadays. So I gave it a go, and I initially enjoyed creating my first digital art using the drawing programs Microsoft Paint and Adobe Illustrator. Because of the digital art techniques utilized, it was a little challenging, but I eventually grew accustomed to it.
