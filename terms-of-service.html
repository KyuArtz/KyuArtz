Terms of Service

  • Accepted Payment Methods: The only accepted payment methods are PayPal, Maya or Gcash.
  • Payment Policy: To avoid potential scams, clients are required to make the payment upfront before receiving the finalized product. Payments must be made within indicated day of agreement.
  • Revision Policy: Revisions are unlimited until the client is satisfied. However, if revisions require significant changes or additional work time, extra charges may apply. Each revision request must be submitted within indicated day of receiving the previous draft.
  • Refund Policy: Refunds are not accepted unless the commission cannot be completed due to unforeseen circumstances on the artist's part.
  • Price Negotiation: Prices can be negotiated based on the client's budget.
  • Right to Reject Commissions: I reserve the right to reject any commission that I find uncomfortable or unsuitable.
  • Intellectual Property Rights: The artist retains all rights to the commissioned artwork. Clients are granted a license to use the artwork for personal purposes. For commercial use, the price will be higher, and the artist must be credited appropriately.
  • Reference Requirements: Clients must provide a clear and detailed reference or description for their commissioned art requests.
  • Progress Updates: I will provide updates on my progress, including sketches, base coloring, and rendering at various stages.
  • Price Adjustments for Complexity: For multiple characters or the addition of a background, the overall price will be doubled from the original quote.
  • Credit Requirement: Clients must credit me when sharing the commissioned work; your acknowledgment is greatly appreciated.
  • Possible Delays: Please anticipate possible delays or tardy responses for your commissioned art request, as I am a working student with multiple clients. Your understanding is appreciated.
  • Prohibition on NFTs and AI: The sale of my commissioned works as NFTs or their use in AI-generated programs is explicitly prohibited. Please refrain from such activities.
  • Delivery Method: The completed commissioned artwork will be delivered via Gmail or Google Drive.
  • Contact Information: If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact me via email at kyuushi992@gmail.com I typically respond within 48 hours.
